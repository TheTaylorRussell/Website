Yo Gabba Gabba

Nick Jr.

At Pixel Pirate Studio, I was part of a team set to animate a short commissioned by popular children’s television program Yo Gabba Gabba.

The staff of the show sent us the audio to be used, a small story about a little boy who liked beets but had to accept that others didn’t like them as much as he did. I worked in a pipeline, first as a keyframe artist, then moved onto in-betweens, and finally cleanups.
