Defense Acquisition University

During my time at Cybermedia Technologies (CTEC), we worked very closely with Defense Acquisition University, a government program designed to help train veterans for a myriad of career paths and technical knowledge.

I was asked to help with the myriad e-learning courses CTEC was in charge of creating everything from infographics, illustrations, motion graphics, stock-photo manipulation, powerpoint presentations, and learning the composition program DART to place all these elements.
