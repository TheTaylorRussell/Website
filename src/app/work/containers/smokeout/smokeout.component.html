Smoke Out

American Cancer Society

The Great American Smokeout was an event held by the American Cancer Society. Its chief focus was to spread awareness of the various methods of quitting smoking, suggesting techniques and lifestyle choices that can be utilized by both the quitter and the people supporting the quitter.

Saxton Moore, Co-owner of Pixel Pirate Studios, commissioned me to work along-side him in crafting a series of eye-catching gifs for the Society to host on their website. These gifs were used as both links and header images for the various quitting/support techniques.

I was given the Illustrator files of each character and whatever props they may have had and I was given a loose idea of what each character should do in the gif they were starring in.
